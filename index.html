Blue Squares Avoidance Game

How to Play:

  • Touch the right side of the screen to move the red square to the right
  • Release to let it move back to the left automatically
  • Avoid touching the falling blue squares
  • The game gets progressively faster as you play
  • Each avoided square gives you 1 point
  • Game ends when you hit a blue square

Tip: Time your movements carefully as the speed increases!
